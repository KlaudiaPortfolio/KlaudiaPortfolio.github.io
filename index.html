Meet Klaudia

Hi! My name is Klaudia Zawadzka, a BA Film student at Falmouth University, and I specialise in Production Design and Cinematography. I've always been interested in all forms of art: from painting and performing to photography and videography. Having tried many forms of art, the latter stuck with me the most. Collaboration is a big part of the film industry, and my course has allowed me to experience that more frequently. Previously, the projects I worked on involved only myself and a handful of actors; now, through the course, I have collaborated with many talented individuals with diverse skills and talents. I can already see improvements within my work and myself as a growing creative in this industry.

I am certain there are skills I can still improve on; however, I hope there will always be. There's perfection in errors, as it gives one a chance to grow.
