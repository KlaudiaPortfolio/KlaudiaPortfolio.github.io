About Me
Hi! My name is Klaudia Zawadzka, a BA Film student at Falmouth university. I've always been interested in all forms of art: from painting and performing to photography and videography. Having tried many forms of art, the latter stuck with me the most. Collaboration is a big part of the film industry and my course has allowed me to experience that more frequently, previously the projects I had worked on involved only myself and the actors. I can already see improvements within my work and myself as a growing creative in this industry.
There are many things I can still improve on, however I hope there always will be. There's perfection in errors as it gives one a chance to grow.
